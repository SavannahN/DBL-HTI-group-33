The sample dataset, preloaded into the tool, contains subway maps from different cities and data collected from several different users. You can use the tool by choosing a map, user, and method(s) from the dataset. Alternatively, you can upload your own data, which will change the map and user options.

These are the stimuli you can choose from.

These are the users from the dataset that data was collected from.
